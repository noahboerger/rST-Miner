This web application provides an implementation of the rST-Miner.
It is a further development of the eST-Miner by Lisa L. Mannel and Wil M. P. van der Aalst.
The given eventlog is converted into partial orders by a concurrency oracle.
Then places are generated randomly and evaluated by the algorithm published at Firing Partial Orders in a Petri Net by R. Bergenthum in PETRI NETS 2021.
